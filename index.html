What Edify Actually Is?

Times have changed and with changing times, the sphere of school education still remains stuck in the past. With these changing times, have we at all, changed? Keeping up with the world and taking into account the concerns of parents and teachers alike, Edify helps you take the next step forward. The integration of our services not only makes our product unique but also gives us an edge over others. So what exactly do we do? Edify provides services that enhance the schooling experience for the students by providing or improving the online services through mobile application and website which are essential for the overall development of the child. We provide mechanisms to parents and teachers which help keep a regular check on the ward’s academic and non-academic progress.

Our Services

Virtual Attendence

There are constant insecurities and concerns the parents have to deal with when their child is away from them, at school. The Edify team understands these concerns and provides methods to solve them. We have come up with a distinctive idea of recording the students movement in and out of their school and buses. Edify will help take virtual attendance of the students in the School and also in the bus, recording each entry and exit movement of theirs.

Live Location

Parents who have their children, going to school and coming home, from the school bus, face a completely different set of concerns. Edify will provide the Live tracking of the school bus which will allow the school authority and parents to track their child’s bus. GPS trackers will be installed in the school buses enabling edify app to project the live location of the bus. The app will indicate the estimated time of arrival of the bus to the pick up or the drop location, so that they do not have to wait unnecessarily at bus stops.

E-report Card

The parents and the teachers’ major interaction is through the students alone. We plan to eliminate this barrier which may cause miscommunication and other problems, by linking the teachers and parents directly, through an app. Edify shall provide parents a constant view at the student’s academic progress. It shall provide the student with constant updates on their academics with E-Report card, and assignments. This will help the school in becoming paperless by sending assignments and report cards online via the Edify app.

Notification and Messeges

A notification and a real time message shall be sent to the parents whenever there is an update in the ward’s activity. This will make school and the parents feel less worried about the child’s activity and whereabouts. The great thing is, this doesn’t require you to have a smartphone and an internet connection to keep your child safe.

Parent - School Interaction

Parent school interaction has suffered for a very long time. We plan to bring an to this huge distance between the two. The Edify app and website will be used as mediums for parents to convey their feedback, complaints and suggestions to the school as well as teachers. This way the school would be able to improve upon where it might be lacking, and would be able to understand the concern of the parents in a better and more wholesome way.
