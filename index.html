What Edify Actually Is?

Times have changed and with changing times, the sphere of school education still remains stuck in the past. With these changing times, have we at all, changed? Keeping up with the world and taking into account the concerns of parents and teachers alike, Edify helps you take the next step forward. The integration of our services not only makes our product unique but also gives us an edge over others. So what exactly do we do? Edify provides services that enhance the schooling experience for the students by providing or improving the online services through mobile application and website which are essential for the overall development of the child. We provide mechanisms to parents and teachers which help keep a regular check on the ward’s academic and non-academic progress.

Virtual Attendence

There are constant insecurities and concerns the parents have to deal with when their child is away from them, at school. The Edify team understands these concerns and provides methods to solve them. We have come up with a distinctive idea of recording the students movement in and out of their school and buses. Edify will help take virtual attendance of the students in the School and also in the bus, recording each entry and exit movement of theirs. Students’ school issued Identity cards will act as the medium to mark the virtual attendance. Parents will be notified about the movement of their child on the Edify app enabling safety and security of the students. This ensures that parents have nothing to worry about, and can send their ward to school more freely than ever before.

Live Location

Parents who have their children, going to school and coming home, from the school bus, face a completely different set of concerns. Edify will provide the Live tracking of the school bus which will allow the school authority and parents to track their child’s bus. GPS trackers will be installed in the school buses enabling edify app to project the live location of the bus. The app will indicate the estimated time of arrival of the bus to the pick up or the drop location, so that they do not have to wait unnecessarily at bus stops. This amps up the safety measures manifolds, as the bus can be completely monitored for its speed, route, estimated time of arrival etc.
